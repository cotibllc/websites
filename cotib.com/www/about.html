Who We Are

COTIB LLC is a New York City-based technology company founded on the belief that businesses deserve straightforward, expert technology guidance — without the jargon, the upsell, or the one-size-fits-all approach.

We started as a consulting firm serving both public and private sector organizations, helping them navigate decisions around cloud computing, cybersecurity, DevOps, and on-premises infrastructure. Over the years, our work exposed us to a common thread: the people doing the work — the IT professionals, the managers, the engineers — rarely had a platform that spoke to their real, day-to-day experience.

That insight led us to expand beyond consulting into content. Today, COTIB operates two content brands that serve the technology and corporate professional communities: The IT XP and Corporate Hardcore. Together, they extend our mission — not just to solve technology problems, but to educate, connect, and give voice to the people living them.

"The first responsibility of a leader is to define reality. The last is to say thank you. In between, the leader is a servant." — Max DePree

That ethos guides everything we do at COTIB — from how we engage with clients to the content we put into the world.
